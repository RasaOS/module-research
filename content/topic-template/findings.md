# Findings — {{TOPIC_TITLE}}

Durable findings: claims that survived scrutiny, each with its evidence and a
confidence note. Distinct from [`log.md`](log.md) (raw activity) — this is the
distilled "what we now believe." Append-only: supersede with a note rather
than silently rewriting. A claim without evidence is an open question, not a
finding.

Cite sources by their local id from [`sources.md`](sources.md) (`[S1]`).
Cross-link related findings in other topics with `@path` (Layer 1) or the
topic with `[[slug]]` (Layer 2).
