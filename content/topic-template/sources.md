stable local id (`S1`, `S2`, …) so [`findings.md`](findings.md) can cite it.
Never reuse an id.

- **First-party / redistributable sources** may be committed alongside this
  topic (`research/{{TOPIC_SLUG}}/sources/`) and referenced by `@path`.
- **Third-party sources** are referenced (URL / citation), not copied.
